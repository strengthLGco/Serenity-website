We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • • Order Fulfillment: Process, ship, and deliver your orders and provide related customer service.
  • • Communication: Send confirmation emails, shipping updates, account notifications, and respond to your inquiries.
  • • Site Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our website functionality, design, and user experience.
  • • Research and Analytics: Understand customer preferences and behavior to improve our product offerings.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • • Payment Processors: We share payment information with PCI-compliant third-party payment processors to process your transactions securely.
  • • Shipping Partners: We share shipping addresses and order details with trusted shipping carriers to deliver your orders.

  • • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, such as in response to court orders or regulatory requests.

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze site usage.

  • • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality, such as age verification and cart management.
  • • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site to improve our services.
  • • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for a personalized experience.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our site.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • • SSL encryption for all data transmitted to and from our website
  • • Secure payment processing through industry-standard payment gateways

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Generally:
