Mechanism of Action

TB-500 is a synthetic version of Thymosin Beta-4, a naturally occurring 43-amino acid peptide found in virtually all human and animal cells. It works by upregulating cell-building proteins like actin, which is involved in cell migration and proliferation. In research settings, TB-500 has shown the ability to promote angiogenesis (new blood vessel formation), reduce inflammation, and accelerate wound healing timelines significantly.
