Frequently Asked Questions

Research peptides are short chains of amino acids designed specifically for laboratory experimentation and scientific study. At Serenity Research Labs, our peptides are synthesized to meet the highest standards of purity for in-vitro research applications.

Every batch undergoes rigorous High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Mass Spectrometry (MS) analysis. We maintain a "Analytical Gallery" standard where every molecule is mapped and verified by independent third-party laboratories.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Apple Pay, and select cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum). All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment infrastructure with end-to-end encryption.

Orders are dispatched within 24 hours in temperature-controlled packaging. We provide global tracked shipping to ensure your research materials arrive in pristine condition, maintaining biological integrity throughout the transit.

Due to the sensitive nature of scientific reagents, we cannot accept returns once the vacuum seal is broken. However, if a product arrives damaged or fails to meet our stated purity standards, we offer immediate replacement or credit.
