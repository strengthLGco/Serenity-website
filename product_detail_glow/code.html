Compound Overview

G-LOW — 70mg total · 3-compound lyophilized research blend. Contains: TB-500 10mg (C₂₁₂H₃₅₀N₅₆O₇₈S, 43-aa Thymosin Beta-4 sequence, actin-binding domain research), BPC-157 10mg (C₆₂H₉₈N₁₆O₂₂, 15-aa gastric pentadecapeptide, cytoprotective signaling research), and GHK-Cu 50mg (C₁₄H₂₄CuN₆O₄, copper-binding tripeptide, ECM remodeling research). This blend combines three compounds with documented activity across distinct but complementary research domains: actin sequestration and cell migration (TB-500), nitric oxide system modulation and gastric mucosal signaling (BPC-157), and collagen biosynthesis and gene expression modulation (GHK-Cu). G-LOW is supplied as a single-vial lyophilized preparation for multi-compound interaction studies and comparative peptide signaling research.

Documented Research Observations

  • check_circle BPC-157: Published research documents molecular mechanisms in gastric cytoprotection, nitric oxide signaling modulation, and extracellular matrix remodeling in in vitro and animal models.
  • check_circle TB-500: Peer-reviewed literature documents actin polymerization regulation, cellular migration enhancement, and immunomodulatory signaling in cell culture and animal research systems.
  • check_circle GHK-Cu: Research publications demonstrate collagen biosynthesis pathways, antioxidant enzyme upregulation, and tissue remodeling signaling mechanisms in laboratory studies.

Extracellular matrix remodeling studies, multi-compound interaction research, synergistic peptide signaling mechanisms, cytoprotection pathway analysis, comparative molecular mechanism investigation, and tissue microenvironment signaling research.
