Mechanism of Action

G-LOW is a potent GLP-1 receptor agonist designed for metabolic research. GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is an incretin hormone naturally released after eating that signals satiety, slows gastric emptying, and enhances insulin secretion. G-LOW mimics this pathway with enhanced stability and duration of action. The 70mg concentration provides researchers with extended study capabilities, making it one of the most cost-effective GLP-1 research compounds available.
