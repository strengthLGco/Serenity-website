## 01 — The Five Content Pillars

### PILLAR 01 — Compound Deep Dives (30%)
Mechanism of action breakdowns, preclinical study summaries, dosing protocols used in published research. Trust-builder. Every compound gets a content series. Lead with science, link to PubMed, never make human efficacy claims.

### PILLAR 02 — Transformation & Results Culture (25%)
The viral engine. Before/after content, "Day X" journey logs, body composition changes, skin glow progressions, recovery timelines. Repost UGC. Create journey content. Always disclaim: "For research/educational purposes. Individual results vary."

### PILLAR 03 — Industry & Regulatory Intel (15%)
2026 reclassification, FDA category changes, compounding pharmacy rulings, market growth data, new study publications. Positions Serenity as a brand that understands the regulatory landscape.

### PILLAR 04 — Founder & Brand Story (15%)
Personal brand. 18-year-old entrepreneur building a research company. Behind-the-scenes. Why you started. Faith-driven entrepreneurship. Gym-to-lab pipeline. Makes people choose Serenity over faceless suppliers.

### PILLAR 05 — Education & Myth-Busting (15%)
How to read a COA. What third-party testing means. Why purity % without a chromatogram is meaningless. "Is this peptide a scam?" response content. Reconstitution tutorials. Storage best practices. Converts skeptics into buyers by teaching quality evaluation — Serenity passes every test you teach them to run.

## Compliance Rails
- No human efficacy claims
- Preclinical framing only ("in rodent models", "in vitro studies suggest")
- PubMed citations required for compound claims
- "For research/educational purposes" disclaimers on transformation content
- "Individual results vary" on any results-adjacent content
- "For Research Use Only — Not for Human Consumption" on product-adjacent posts
