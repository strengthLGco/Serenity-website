Mechanism of Action

BPC-157 — C₆₂H₉₈N₁₆O₂₂ · MW 1419.53 Da · CAS 137525-51-0. A synthetic pentadecapeptide consisting of 15 amino acids (Gly-Glu-Pro-Pro-Pro-Gly-Lys-Pro-Ala-Asp-Asp-Ala-Gly-Leu-Val), derived from a partial sequence of human gastric juice body protection compound. BPC-157 has been investigated in preclinical models for its role in nitric oxide (NO) system modulation, FAK-paxillin pathway activation, and VEGF-mediated angiogenic signaling. Research applications include cytoprotective signaling studies, gastric mucosal integrity models, and extracellular matrix remodeling investigations in cell culture and animal systems.
